What I Do

I work with development teams who know they could be shipping faster. Whether it's introducing AI-powered workflows, levelling up mobile development practices, or building the automation that compliance demands — I embed with your team, set things up properly, and step back.

AI-Powered Development

Your developers are curious about AI but nobody's figured out how to use it productively. I've shipped production code with AI assistants daily for years. I'll introduce the right tools, establish quality gates, set up AI-powered code reviews and collaboration workflows, and get your team from experimenting to delivering.

Mobile Development

7+ years of React Native across health tech, mobility platforms, and agency work. I audit app architectures, pair with your developers, and help teams build apps that genuinely work on both platforms. Not advice from the sideline — hands-on.

Automated Testing & Compliance

Fully automated testing pipelines. Regression testing, compatibility testing, deployment gates. For teams working under strict compliance and regulation demands, I build the workflow automation that gives you confidence every release meets the bar — without slowing you down.

CI/CD & Workflow Automation

Build pipelines that work, code review culture that doesn't slow you down, shipping rhythms that stick. I set up the infrastructure and habits, then step back. No 200-page process documents — just things that work.
